Privacy Policy

ProteinFirst

Your privacy is our priority. ProteinFirst is designed with privacy at its core. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application. Simply put: we don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

ProteinFirst is built to respect your privacy completely. The app collects only the nutrition tracking data you choose to enter, and this information never leaves your device. The data you provide includes:

  • Protein Intake Logs: Daily protein amounts you manually enter
  • Food Information: Food names and protein content you search for or calculate
  • Daily Goals: Your personal protein targets and preferences
  • App Settings: Display preferences and customization options

Important: We do NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials (no registration required)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Health data beyond what you manually enter
  • Contact lists or social media information
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Analytics or usage data

2. How We Collect Information

All data entry is completely voluntary and transparent. You are in full control:

  • You manually enter protein intake amounts
  • You choose what foods to search for in the database
  • You set your own daily protein goals
  • You customize app settings according to your preferences

The app never collects information automatically or in the background. Every piece of data is something you actively provide through the app interface.

3. How We Use Your Information

The nutrition data you enter is used exclusively to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Track Progress: Display your daily protein intake and progress toward goals
  • Calculate Nutrition: Help you find protein content of foods using the built-in database
  • Visualize Data: Show progress bars and daily logs of your intake
  • Save Preferences: Remember your settings and customization choices

Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than providing you with protein tracking functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All your nutrition data is stored exclusively on your iPhone. We do not transmit, upload, sync, or store your information on any external servers, cloud services, or databases. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • You maintain complete control over your information
  • No one else can access your nutrition data
  • No internet connection is required to use the app
  • Your privacy is protected by default

Data Protection

We implement security best practices to protect your locally stored data:

  • Secure local storage using iOS data protection
  • No external network communications
  • No third-party integrations that could access your data
  • Regular security updates through app updates

Please Note: Since all data is stored on your device, you are responsible for your device's security. We recommend using Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode, keeping iOS updated, and backing up your device regularly through iCloud or iTunes.

5. No Registration Required

ProteinFirst does not require any user registration, account creation, or login. This means:

  • No email address or personal information is collected
  • No passwords to remember or manage
  • No user accounts that could be compromised
  • You can start using the app immediately after installation

This design choice ensures maximum privacy and convenience for all users.

6. Data Retention

Your nutrition data remains on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. You have complete control over data retention:

  • Delete individual protein log entries at any time
  • Clear all data through the app settings if available
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data

Once data is deleted or the app is uninstalled, the information is permanently removed and cannot be recovered by us or anyone else.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Zero Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, trade, or transfer your nutrition data to any third parties under any circumstances. Your information remains completely private.

We do NOT:

  • Share data with advertising networks
  • Sell information to data brokers
  • Provide data to analytics companies
  • Share information with social media platforms
  • Transfer data to cloud services
  • Disclose information for marketing purposes
  • Use third-party tracking tools

No Third-Party Services

ProteinFirst operates completely independently without any third-party integrations:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage or sync services
  • No social media integrations
  • No external APIs that transmit your data

8. Advertising

ProteinFirst is completely ad-free. We do not display advertisements, which means:

  • No ad networks have access to any data
  • No tracking pixels or cookies
  • Your experience is never interrupted by ads
  • No targeted advertising based on your nutrition data
  • No data collection for ad personalization

9. External Links

The app may contain links to external resources, such as nutritional information websites. Please be aware:

  • External sites are not operated by us
  • We have no control over their content or privacy practices
  • Their privacy policies may differ from ours
  • You should review their privacy policies before providing information

You maintain complete control over your nutrition data:

  • View Your Data: Access all your logs at any time
  • Edit Information: Modify any entries as needed
  • Delete Data: Remove individual entries or all data
  • Export Data: Your data stays on your device and can be backed up through iOS

12. Data Breaches

Because all data is stored only on your device and never transmitted anywhere, the risk of data breaches through our app is virtually zero. We have:

  • No servers that could be hacked
  • No databases that could be breached
  • No network communications that could be intercepted
  • No cloud storage that could be compromised

Your data security depends primarily on your device security practices.

  • Your data never crosses borders
  • Regional privacy laws are inherently respected
